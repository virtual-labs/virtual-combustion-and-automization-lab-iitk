Objectives of Virtual Combustion and Atomization (VCA) Lab:



  • To provide access to the advanced combustion experimentations to both the under-graduate and post-graduate students of other colleges in India.

  • To expose the students and motivate them by creating an opportunity to the advanced experiments which are usually not available for other engineering colleges.

  • To incorporate animations of underlying process and intricate phenomena during experimentations to make the student appreciate and develop better understanding of the fundamental concepts.

  • Hence, through this virtual lab, the students from other engineering colleges will be benefited both conceptually and practically.
